Great environment to practice the language and make lots of Japanese friends!

Langchange is a free and user-friendly website
language exchange focused on English and Japanese

Langchange is a language exchange website specialized for Japanese. Since we are Japanese students, we know how Japanese feel, and it would be easier for Japanese people to use, more secure, and more fun, compared with other language exchange websites. There are many Japanese users at here at Langchange.

Language exchange happens when two people who practice each other's language by talking to each other in both languages. For example, an English speaker who is learning Japanese will do a language exchange with a Japanese speaker who is learning English. You can have fun learning language with your friends. ...You don't like studying? This is not "studying". The best way to improve your language skill is to use it. Just having fun talking in Japanese with friends will help you a lot.
