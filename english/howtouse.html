How It Works

①Book a Talk
Your partner will be automatically matched.

At "Talk in Japanese", book a talk just by clicking a "book" button at your available time. If you're a begginer, you can choose a Japanese partner that can speak English. Then, read "Talk Guidelines" to prepare for the talk.

②Let's talk in Japanese!

Click "Stand by" button at Main Page to call your partner. Video window will show up and automatically start the call once both of you are ready. You can also practice Japanese using video and chat box. The talk lasts for 25 minutes. After that, your LC coin will be sent to the partner.

③Help learn English in return!

At "Talk in English", click "Open" at your available time and wait for the matching. This time you can help a Japanese person learn English. You can get an LC coin after each talk in English.

④Make more friends!

At "History" on "Friends", you can see the people whom you've talked with. If you find a person whom you get along with, you can just check his/her schedule and book "talks" with him/her again. After speaking to each other in both languages, you can be friends with each other. Let's make a lot of friends!
