Why Langchange?

Security systems for preventing and blocking trouble users.

Japanese people value security.
Our well-secured system will get more Japanese people to join Langchange, which will improve your learning environment. There are some features that we have for your security. You will not see your partner until the video chat starts. Also, if you meet a partner who bothers you a lot, you can simply block him/her.

Just 1 click for reservation. Our time-based matching system is new and the easiest.

How many steps do you usually have in a language exchange? You look for and choose a partner, and exchange messages to decide a schedule. A ton of tiring steps are waiting for you. Langchange gives you an easier option. You only need to click "book" at your available time. That's it. The system automatically look for your partner. Isn't it amazing?

Free service and online video chat make it easy to start and keep your motivation!

You don't want to pay money for talking with Japanese people. it doesn't need money to talk to people, right? For students, our video chat service is for free. It's super easy for new users to start and keep their motivation. At home, at a cafe, in a bathroom...anywhere you can enjoy conversations at Langchange.

Thoughtful support for you

We prepared materials and guidelines for you to be successful in learning the language. You can choose Japanese partners who can speak English. Also, for those of you who are not very comfortable in speaking a foreign language, there are some guidelines to help you get started. Our unique friend system will also help yo make more and more friends who can support your language learning.

Since your partners are also learning foreign languages, they share the same feeling and are willing to help you learn Japanese.

Our Passion

Langchange was born from a voice of an exchange student in Japan. He said that there is hardly a chance for people from foreign countries to learn Japanese here in Japan since it is quite difficult for them to interact with the locals.

In Japan, there will be more and more people from foreign countries. It's pretty easy to find English in any town. We know that there are people who want to study Japanese. But because Japanese people are shy, it's kind of hard for them to talk to someone who speaks a different language.

On the other hand, Japanese have a problem in learning English. They focus on reading but not so much on speaking. Because of that, Japanese people cam read high level academic essays, but somehow they can barely talk in English. We think this is a serious problem. They need to practice English conversation more.

What if people from foreign countries can connect with Japanese who want to study English...?

The mission of Langchange is "Change language learning". We are more than happy if Langchange can make your learning better.

There will be much excitement when you make friends at Langchange.

Inneed, Student Organization
